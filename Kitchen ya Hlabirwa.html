Welcome to MTYK

MTYK is an urban agri-business based in Innisfree Park, Northen suburbs of Sandton.
Founded in 2013 by Lekau Nkoko, we specialize in organic produce cultivation and agro-processing, promoting a healthy lifestyle to consumers.
MTYK is run by a complementary mother-daughter duo: Lekau Nkoko serves as the heard farmer and agro-processor,while her daughter Motushi Nkoko manages the day-to-day business operations.

About Page

The farm used to be a cow kraal before the owner of the land donated the entire park to the city of Johannesburg.
The farm sits on a chunk of 2.5 hectares, the land is utilized to grow organic produce(vegetables and herbs), we also have a varienty of fruits trees(70 fruit tress).
Innesfree Park has amazing fertile soil which allows MTYK to organically grow their vast vegetation which grows in a healthy eco-system with a variety of insects which have made the soil their home.
MTYK believes that the soil should be left to be processed naturally by insects with a little enhancement from some natural mulch .

Benefits of Natural Mulch


1.Helps protect the soil
2.Helps with moisture retention.
MTYK places emphasis on native,perennial plants, which are loved and adored by local insect populations.
Innesfree Farm is a water land in its own right so we ultimately do not waste water during production.
We have a wet land in the heart of the land, a river on the outer edges(near the farming land) and multiple clean water connections.
